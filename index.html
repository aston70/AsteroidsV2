Asteroids Game Help Guide - Version 2

Objective

Your mission is to eliminate the enemy ship before it destroys all the asteroids. The ship is quick, so you need to be fast and precise. You control a UFO with similar movement to the ship, and your task is to prevent the ship from completing its mission.

You can only fire in short bursts, and your bullets will always fire in the direction the ship is pointing.

Controls

Movement

  • ↑Thrust forward
  • ←Rotate left
  • →Rotate right

Shooting

  • Space Fire bullets in the direction of the UFO's rotation

Scoring

  • Hit the ship with a bullet: 75 points
  • UFO collides with the ship (one time per round): 500 points
  • Destroy the ship: 100 points
  • Remaining asteroids after the ship is destroyed: 7 bonus points per asteroid

Dangers & Health

  • UFO touch with the ship: Reduces ship health by 50%
  • Ship's attacks: The ship's will attack you if you get close. Each hit reduces health 20%
  • Game over: when the ship destroys all asteroids or when your health reaches 0%

Tips for Success

  • Be quick! The ship can destroy asteroids fast, so time is of the essence.
  • Don't sit still.
  • Fire as many rounds as you can.
  • Touch the ship once to reduce its health by 50%, but avoid direct collisions.
  • Focus on taking out the ship before it destroys too many asteroids.

Good luck, pilot!
